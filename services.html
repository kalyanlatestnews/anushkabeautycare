Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

Do you provide makeup trial for brides?

Yes, we offer makeup trials for brides. We recommend booking a trial session 1-2 weeks before your wedding day to finalize the look. The trial cost can be adjusted in the final bridal package.

What products do you use for treatments?

We use premium branded products from trusted companies like Lakme, VLCC, Shahnaz Husain, and other reputed beauty brands to ensure the best results and safety for our clients.

How far in advance should I book for bridal makeup?

We recommend booking your bridal makeup at least 2-3 months in advance, especially during wedding season. This ensures availability and allows time for trials and consultations.

Do you offer home service for bridal makeup?

Yes, we provide home service for bridal makeup within Imamganj and nearby areas. Additional charges apply for travel and transportation. Please contact us for more details.

What is your cancellation policy?

Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled with 24 hours notice. For bridal bookings, advance payment is required and cancellation terms may vary. Please discuss with us during booking.

Are your tools and equipment sanitized?

Absolutely! We maintain strict hygiene standards. All tools and equipment are properly sanitized before each use. We also use disposable items wherever possible to ensure maximum safety.
